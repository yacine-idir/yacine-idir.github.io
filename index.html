Spatio-Temporal Geostatistics

Variogram modeling, kriging techniques, and covariance function design for joint space-time processes. Expertise in handling irregular sampling and non-stationary phenomena.

Kriging Variogram Covariance ST

VecchiaCensored — R Package

An open-source R package for efficient spatial estimation and prediction with censored data. Implements both frequentist and Bayesian approaches using the Vecchia approximation, supporting spatially varying coefficient models and classical Gaussian processes on large-scale datasets.
